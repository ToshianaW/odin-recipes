Spaghetti Recipe

You simply cannot go wrong with spaghetti. It is super easy and a true Italian classic leaving you full and satisfied. The next best thing is to pair it with a glass of wine!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef, Turkey or any vegan meat alternative
  • Ragu Super Chunky Mushroom Sauce
  • Your choice of spaghetti noodles
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Set your pot to a boil, add a tsp of salt
  2. Once boiling, add your spaghetti noodles, let boil for 12 minutes
  3. While spaghetti is boiling, put meat on frying pan and fry for 15min, stir regularly
  4. Once spaghetti is done, drain the water
  5. Add meat to spaghetti and then add the Ragu Sauce, stir until completely mixed
  6. Place on plate and add Parmesean Cheese. Enjoy!
