Taco Recipe

Return

Tacos are a world classic. With this Mexican-inspired meal, you can have your taste buds run wild with imagination. This savory recipe will leave you asking for more!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef, Turkey or Vegan Meat Alternatives
  • Shredded Chedder Cheese
  • Chilli Powder Seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Picante Sauce
  • Sour Cream
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Place Ground Beef on skillet and let fry for 15 minutes
  2. Chop lettuce is to quarter sizes
  3. Use chilli powder seasoning on meat and mix
  4. Warm tortillas on high pan and flip continuosly for 45 seconds
  5. Once meat is done, place on tortilla and add your condiments
  6. Add Lettuce, Sour Cream, Chedder Cheese, Picante Sauce and Enjoy!
